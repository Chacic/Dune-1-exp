Exclusive Zero-Gravity Games

Dive into unique experiences only possible in space. Our innovative games make the most of zero gravity for unforgettable fun.

Gravity Ball

**Gravity Ball** is an exhilarating sport designed specifically for the zero-gravity environment of our space station. The game takes place in a spacious room lined with metal handrails extending from the walls. These handrails are essential, as they allow players to navigate the weightless environment by pushing and pulling themselves in any direction. Two teams compete, each aiming to score by placing a ball—similar in size to a standard football—into the opposing team's hoop located at opposite ends of the room. The lack of gravity adds a thrilling challenge, requiring players to strategize their movements, anticipate trajectories, and work closely with teammates. Quick reflexes and spatial awareness are key, making Gravity Ball an unforgettable experience that blends physical activity with the unique sensation of floating freely in space.

Space Golf

**Space Golf** reimagines the classic game of golf, adapting it to the captivating realm of zero gravity. Set on a specially designed mini-golf course within our station, traditional golf clubs are replaced with sophisticated controlled air guns. These devices allow players to direct bursts of air to propel the golf ball, offering precise control over power and direction. Navigating the course's intricate obstacles becomes an engaging challenge, as the absence of gravity means the ball doesn't simply roll—it floats and glides through the air. Players must adjust their strategies, considering three-dimensional movement and the unique physics of space. Space Golf provides a fun and innovative way to enjoy a beloved sport, offering both novice and experienced golfers a chance to test their skills in an entirely new context.

Flying Ball

**Flying Ball** is another of our exclusive zero-gravity games, where players don RCS (Reaction Control System) backpacks to maneuver through a three-dimensional arena. The objective is to navigate the space and place a ball into designated target areas, competing either individually or in teams. The RCS backpacks, similar to those used by astronauts during spacewalks, allow for precise movement in all directions. This game challenges players to master spatial orientation, coordination, and timing. The thrill of gliding effortlessly while competing adds to the excitement, making Flying Ball a highlight of our entertainment offerings.
