Exclusive Zero-Gravity Games

Dive into unique experiences only possible in space. Our innovative games make the most of zero gravity for unforgettable fun.

Space Golf

Navigate a challenging obstacle course, guiding a golf ball with air guns. Precision and control are key in this zero-gravity sport.

Graviball

Use wall-mounted rods to maneuver and score goals in mid-air. Teamwork and agility define this exhilarating game.

Flying Ball

Master your RCS backpack to navigate three-dimensional space, aiming to place the ball in target zones. Strategy meets skill in this spatial adventure.
